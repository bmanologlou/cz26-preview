Our Purpose

INJECTION
SERVICE
REDEFINED.

Modern fuel injection systems operate at pressures exceeding 2800 bar, tolerances measured in microns, and software complexity that demands more than a conventional bench can offer.

Carbon Zapp exists because the market needed equipment that matches this reality — not yesterday's tools adapted for today's engines, but purpose-built systems engineered from the ground up for Diesel and Gasoline injection specialists who demand precision without compromise.

From a single-cylinder CRDi injector to a marine 2-stroke Wärtsilä engine — we build the bench that gets it right.
